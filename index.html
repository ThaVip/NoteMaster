About NoteMaster

The inspiration for NoteMaster came from my own need to keep my thoughts, ideas, and tasks organized. I found myself constantly switching between different note-taking applications and losing track of where I stored my notes. I wanted a simple, secure, and efficient solution that would help me stay organized in one place.

The development of NoteMaster started as a personal project, and over time it evolved into a more robust application. This project is part of my portfolio for ALX SE, where I am learning full-stack development.
